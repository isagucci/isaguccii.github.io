NYCTOPHOBIA

Nyctophobia is an extreme fear of the dark.
The name comes from the Greek word for night. Children and adults with nyctophobia may fear
being alone in the dark. They may have anxiety in dark places, and they may have
trouble sleeping in a darkened room.
