SCOPOHOBIA










Scopophobia is an excessive fear of being stared at. While it is not unusual to feel anxious or
uncomfortable in situations where you’re likely to be the center of attention — like performing or
speaking publicly — scopophobia is more severe. It can feel as though you’re being scrutinized.

Like other phobias, the fear is out of proportion to the risk involved. In fact, the anxiety can
become so intense that it can keep you from functioning in social situations, including school and
work.
